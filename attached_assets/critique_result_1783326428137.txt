1. Reference Style Benchmark

The SocialLab website style is:

Mood: warm AI research lab, social-good innovation, academic but modern.
Colors: sand/beige canvas, deep teal structure, coral/salmon emphasis, peach/orange warmth, off-white breathing sections.
Typography: condensed, geometric, technical, narrow, lightly futuristic. Headings have wide tracking.
Layout: very spacious, horizontal, presentation-like sections with large gaps and strong visual rhythm.
Components: fixed navbar, coral active underline, rounded pill buttons, icon rows, stat bands, soft flat cards.
Illustrations: custom SVG-like diagrams, human/brain/data/network motifs, circular arcs, node dots, thin lines.
Motion direction: should feel slow, calm, precise, like diagrams assembling.

This should not become a generic SaaS landing page.
