# Content Rules for Replit

When inserting this content into the existing website:

1. Do not redesign the website.
2. Keep the current visual style, layout system, typography, colors, navbar, and progress rail.
3. Replace placeholder text only.
4. Keep each slide focused on one idea.
5. Use short headlines and concise copy.
6. Use maximum 3–4 bullets per slide.
7. Avoid long paragraphs.
8. Do not invent facts.
9. Do not use unsupported numbers.
10. Do not claim “open-source” unless verified.
11. Prefer “public frameworks, platforms, and initiatives.”
12. Use project names only if public and verified.
13. Keep the tone professional, human-centered, and technical but simple.
14. Make content fit the existing slide layouts.
15. Keep mobile readable and concise.

# Recommended Final Narrative

The presentation should position SocialLab as a human-centered AI ecosystem.

The clearest story is:

**SocialLab is an AI Innovation Factory and Academy that combines AI, data science, social sciences, public frameworks, platforms, training, and impact-driven projects to help organizations use AI responsibly in real-world domains.**

The presentation should move from identity to purpose, then capabilities, method, public initiatives, proof, impact areas, and closing call-to-action.
