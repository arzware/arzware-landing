## What it is
Project Overview The “Disinfo Demasked” project addresses the global threat of information disorders affecting countries in the Global South. This initiative focuses on analyzing propaganda and disinformation targeting the Arabic-speaking world. By leveraging AI and LLMs, the project unveils the underlying narratives in these disinformation campaigns.
