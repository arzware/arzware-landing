## What it is
The Maharat Foundation in Lebanon steps up to this challenge with their innovative initiative for the 2022 elections – a comprehensive, open data dashboard designed to monitor the elections with an interactive and user-friendly approach. Project Overview This initiative introduces a sophisticated dashboard that offers open data features, enabling users to access, navigate, and interact with election-related data. The design is intuitively crafted, focusing on ease of use while providing deep insights into the election process.
