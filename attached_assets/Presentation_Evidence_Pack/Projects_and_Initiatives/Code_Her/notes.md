## One-line description
Code-Her is an advanced Data Journalism course and learning material designed by SocialLab and Deutsche Welle Akademie to strengthen female journalist in the MENA region.

## What it is
The project qualified young female journalists in the MENA region in data journalism. 26 young aspiring journalists managed to break into the field of data journalism and submit their own data story at the end of a comprehensive and challenging 14-week long training. We would like to share our gathered data knowledge with you on this platform.
