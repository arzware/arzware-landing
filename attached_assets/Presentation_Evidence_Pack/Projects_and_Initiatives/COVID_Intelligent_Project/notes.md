## What it is
It is an interactive dashboard developed to help the world understand different insights about the COVID pandemic to manage, analyze, and monitor public discourse on COVID topics during the pandemic. Users are able to use the dashboard to build, filter, design, or export custom stories and aggregations based on their research. The project uses ML and NLP technologies on more than 70 different languages and dialects and almost 1 billion tweets with an open-sourced methodology that’s shared openly to support research articles.
